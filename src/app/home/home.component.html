Our project is about seeking the space ships and satellites. The project is a web application that will helps the users to search the past, present, and future rocket launches through a 3D map, and we have a filter to locate the ships and satellites easier. Finally, we have a function called Picture of the Day which could show us some pictures and articles from NASA.
